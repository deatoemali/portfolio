Project Task

While learning how to use Maya 2024 from knowing nothing, I am tasked to think about an animation narrative to animate using Maya 2024 or other 3D modelling softwares.

Goals

Create a short fully-renderred 3D animation that reflects the materials taught during the semester.

My Impact

  • Designed a short animation narrative and successfully illustrated the character concept and storyboard

  • Using Autodesk Maya 2024, delivered various 3D models of characters and environment for the animation with neat and proper topography

  • Navigated the character and environment models and lighting with Maya 2024 and successfully aligned the shots and sound effects into one video

Key Takeaways

  • Learned how to model low-poly characters, environments, and real-life objects using Autodesk Maya 2024 from scratch.
